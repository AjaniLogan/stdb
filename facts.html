WHAT IS GONORRHEA(THE CLAP)?

Gonorrhea is a sexually transmitted bacterial infection that, if untreated, may cause infertility.

Symptoms

*There sometimes will be no symptoms* - Painful Urination - Vaginal Bleeding after sex 1- Abdominal or Pelvic Pain
